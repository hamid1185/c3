Ethical Considerations & Disclaimers

Important Notice

The Indigenous Art Atlas is committed to respecting Indigenous cultures, knowledge, and heritage. This platform operates under strict ethical guidelines to ensure cultural sensitivity and appropriate representation of Indigenous art.

Cultural Sensitivity

We recognize that Indigenous art carries deep cultural, spiritual, and historical significance. Our platform is designed with the following principles:

  • Respect for sacred and ceremonial art that may not be appropriate for public display
  • Recognition that some locations hold cultural significance and should not be publicly disclosed
  • Acknowledgment of Indigenous peoples' rights to their cultural heritage and intellectual property
  • Commitment to accurate and respectful representation of Indigenous cultures and traditions

Location Sensitivity

The Indigenous Art Atlas takes location privacy seriously:

  • Users can mark submissions as "culturally sensitive" or "private land" to protect exact locations
  • Sensitive locations are displayed only as general regions, never exact coordinates
  • Our moderation team reviews all location data to ensure appropriate disclosure levels
  • We encourage users to exercise caution when submitting location information

Permission for Contemporary Art

When submitting contemporary Indigenous art:

  • Only submit artwork you have permission to photograph and share
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights of living artists
  • Ensure you have appropriate permissions for gallery, museum, or privately-owned works
  • Credit artists appropriately and accurately whenever possible
  • Do not submit work from private collections without explicit permission

Non-Commercial Purpose

The Indigenous Art Atlas operates as a non-commercial, educational platform:

  • This platform is designed for documentation, education, and cultural preservation
  • We do not sell, license, or commercially exploit submitted content
  • Users retain rights to their submitted content
  • The platform should not be used for commercial gain or exploitation

Content Moderation

All submissions undergo review to ensure:

  • Accuracy of information provided
  • Appropriateness of content for public viewing
  • Respect for cultural sensitivities and protocols
  • Compliance with legal requirements regarding copyright and permissions

User Responsibilities

By using this platform, users agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Respect Indigenous cultures and communities
  • Obtain necessary permissions before photographing or submitting artwork
  • Report any inappropriate or inaccurate content
  • Use the platform for educational and documentary purposes only

Limitation of Liability

The Indigenous Art Atlas and its administrators:

  • Cannot verify the accuracy of all submitted information
  • Are not responsible for user-generated content
  • Reserve the right to remove any content deemed inappropriate or inaccurate
  • Make no warranties regarding the completeness or accuracy of information

Contact & Concerns

If you have concerns about content on this platform, including:

  • Culturally inappropriate material
  • Inaccurate information
  • Copyright violations
  • Location sensitivity issues

Please use our content reporting feature on each artwork page, or contact us directly at IndegeniousArtAtlas@gmail.com
